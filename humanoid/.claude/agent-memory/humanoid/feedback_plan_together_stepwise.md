**Never bulk-author planning artifacts (OpenSpec proposal/design/tasks, roadmaps) in one solo
burst. Plan together, step by step — one artifact, often one section/decision at a time, discussed
before moving to the next.**

**Why:** Anton, 2026-07-13, after I wrote proposal.md + design.md + tasks.md for the locbench
change in three rapid solo writes: "wait wait wait, we plan together, you have no idea, lets move
together step by step." He is the architect; a pre-baked plan removes his decision points and
assumes context I don't have. A locked design conversation ≠ license to expand it into full
artifacts alone — the expansion itself contains dozens of micro-decisions that are his.

**How to apply:** in any planning flow (/manager, opsx:propose, research specs): present the next
smallest unit (scope statement, one design decision, one task section) in chat, get his read,
THEN write it down. Writing files comes after agreement, not before. Related:
[[feedback-manager-plans-not-builds]], [[feedback-explain-plainly]].

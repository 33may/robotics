The humanoid project’s summer 2026 target is a working end-of-summer demo, not only a foundation document. The main demo focuses on **reasoning**: put Oli in a new/prepared environment, have it walk around, build/update a semantic map, detect people, communicate with them, and explain what it sees/where it is/what it plans to do. The **acting** track runs in parallel as research on model structure/orchestration, humanoid data collection, world models/synthetic data generation, and fine-tuning; a small real hand manipulation task is optional depending on research results.

As of 2026-06-15, Oli is repaired and physically available, but the first week should still be simulation-first with limited real-robot onboarding and capability checks.

**Why:** The user wants the project to produce a concrete demo by end of August 2026 while keeping acting research active but not blocking the reasoning demo; sim-first work reduces hardware risk while Oli is newly available again.

**How to apply:** When planning humanoid work before September 2026, prioritize reasoning-demo milestones and treat acting/manipulation as a parallel research/proof-point track unless the user says the acting results are ready to integrate. For Week 1 planning, include some physical Oli inspection/testing, but make Isaac Sim and sim asset setup the main focus.
